Experimental Facilities

SAF-EAGLE experimental activities are supported by advanced laboratory infrastructure for fuel characterization, engine testing and emissions analysis.

Engine testing platform for steady-state testing

Controlled setup for analysing engine performance, fuel consumption and emissions.

AVL test bench for steady-state and transient engine testing

Instrumentation for precise control and measurement of fuel flow, enabling accurate experimental analysis.

Engine instrumentation and EVI system

High-resolution sensors and EVI system for pressure, temperature and flow measurements across different engine sections.

Fuel characterization laboratory

Laboratory equipment for measuring density, viscosity, calorific value and other key fuel properties.

Aerospace systems testing

Facilities for evaluating elastomeric material behaviour and degradation, with emerging capabilities for aerospace system testing.

Real-time emissions measurement system

OBS-ONE system for high-precision, real-time measurement of exhaust gas emissions, enabling detailed analysis of pollutants during engine operation under transient conditions.

Micro-jet engine test bench

Experimental setup used to evaluate engine performance, fuel consumption and emissions under controlled laboratory conditions.

Altitude simulation and environmental control system

HORIBA-based test system capable of simulating altitude conditions up to 3000 m, with precise control of temperature and humidity for experimental evaluation of engine behaviour under varying atmospheric conditions.
